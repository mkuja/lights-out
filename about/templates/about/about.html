LightsOut -Discord bot

Project purpose

Purpose of the project is, of course, to mess around with the lights in your home. Please register, and have our Discord -bot on your server to watch for mentions of registered people. It will play cool light-effects in your home whenever someone mentions you. Of course, you can also have the bot also ignore people who think they can make a disco of your house.

At the moment only LIFX -brand lights work.

How to get stuff working

First you need to buy some LIFX-brand color lights. Register to LIFX and install the lights as per provided instruction. Almost lastly, get your LIFX developer token from https://cloud.lifx.com. Then register to LightsOut -website, provide your Discord-tag and paste your developer token to the app in "Edit yourself" -menu.

Bot commands

Bot commands are as follows.

  • $enable Enables light effects on the server for you.
  • $disable Disables light effects on the server for you.
  • $ignore user Disables light effects from mentions made by user.
  • $unignore user Re-enables light effects from mentions made by user.
  • $ignore_list Gives list of your ignored Discord users.
  • $guilds Shows your enabled guilds (aka servers).
Bot is disabled for each user on a guild by default (even if they're registered) and user has to enable it first.
