Lights out -Discord bot

Project purpose

Purpose of the project is, of course, to mess around with the lights in your home. Please register, and have our Discord -bot on your server to watch for mentions of registered people. It will play cool light-effects in your home whenever someone mentions you. Of course, you can also have the bot also ignore people who think they can make a disco of your house.

At the moment only LIFX -brand lights work.

How to get stuff working

First you need to buy some LIFX-brand color lights. Register to LIFX and install the lights as per provided instruction. Almost lastly, get your LIFX developer token from https://cloud.lifx.com. Then register to Lights out -website, provide your Discord-tag and paste your developer token to the app in "Edit yourself" -menu.

Bot commands

Bot commands are as follows.

  • $enable Enables light effects on the server for you.
  • $enable_global Enables light effects on every server you share for you
  • $disable Disables light effects on the server for you.
  • $disable_global Disables light effects globally for you.
  • $ignore_from user Disables light effects from mentions made by user.
  • $ignore_channel channel Ignores mentions on channel. The command must be given on the server the channel is on.
  • $unignore_channel channel Unignores channel. The command must be given on the server the channel is on.
  • $my_channels Shows your shared, ignored and enabled channels.
Disabling the bot globally is essentially same as ignoring every channel on every server you share. To allow effects from just some channels you can disable it globally and then unignore wanted channels.
